rogues.matrices.prolate¶

rogues.matrices.prolate(n, w=0.25)¶
PROLATE Prolate matrix - symmetric, ill-conditioned Toeplitz matrix.

A = PROLATE(N, W) is the N-by-N prolate matrix with parameter W. It is a symmetric Toeplitz matrix. If 0 < W < 0.5 then

  • A is positive definite
  • the eigenvalues of A are distinct, lie in (0, 1), and tend to cluster around 0 and 1.

W defaults to 0.25.

Reference: J.M. Varah. The Prolate matrix. Linear Algebra and Appl., 187:269–278, 1993.
