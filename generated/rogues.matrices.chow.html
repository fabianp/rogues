rogues.matrices.chow¶

rogues.matrices.chow(n, alpha=1, delta=0)¶
chow(n, alpha, delta): chow matrix - a singular toeplitz

lower hessenberg matrix. a = chow(n, alpha, delta) is a toeplitz lower hessenberg matrix a = h(alpha) + delta*eye, where h(i,j) = alpha^(i-j+1). h(alpha) has p = floor(n/2) zero eigenvalues, the rest being 4*alpha*cos( k*pi/(n+2) )^2, k=1:n-p. defaults: alpha = 1, delta = 0.

References: T.S. Chow, A class of Hessenberg matrices with known

eigenvalues and inverses, SIAM Review, 11 (1969), pp. 391-395.
  1. Fairweather, On the eigenvalues and eigenvectors of a class of Hessenberg matrices, SIAM Review, 13 (1971), pp. 220-221.
