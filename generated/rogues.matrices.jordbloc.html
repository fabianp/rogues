rogues.matrices.jordbloc¶

rogues.matrices.jordbloc(n, lambduh=1)¶
jordbloc jordan block.
jordbloc(n, lambda) is the n-by-n jordan block with eigenvalue lambduh. lambduh = 1 is the default. (Recall that lambda is a reserved word in Python)
