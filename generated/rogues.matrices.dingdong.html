rogues.matrices.dingdong¶

rogues.matrices.dingdong(n)¶
dingdong dingdong matrix - a symmetric Hankel matrix.
a = dingdong(n) is the symmetric n-by-n Hankel matrix with
a(i,j) = 0.5/(n-i-j+1.5).

the eigenvalues of a cluster around pi/2 and -pi/2.

Invented by F.N. Ris.

Reference: J.C. Nash, Compact Numerical Methods for Computers: Linear Algebra and Function Minimisation, second edition, Adam Hilger, Bristol, 1990 (Appendix 1).
